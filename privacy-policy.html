Privacy Policy

Here at Clipping Path Studio, we appreciate and understand the importance of individual
privacy.

We aim to protect our clients’ personal data and files to ensure smooth, secure, and comfortable business relationships.

Please read our Privacy Policy to learn how our company collects and uses data obtained from the website while protecting our clients’ integrity.

Feel free to contact us at info@pictureclipingpath.com for your questions about our Privacy Policy.

Collection of Client Information

By using the Clipping Path Studio website, you allow us to collect the following personal information:

  • First and last name
  • Email address, phone numbers, and other contact details
  • Company name and if applicable, the VAT number
  • Job title and occupation
  • Country and location

We may auto-collect several system information such as your web browser version and type, browsing history, operating system type, IP address, and others.

We do not collect or store your billing and payment information, including your credit and debit card details.

Please be advised that this website is intended for users aged 13 and above. Clipping Path Studio does not knowingly collect any personal data from children under the age of 13.

The Use of Client Data

Our company needs accurate client information so we can provide you with efficient, seamless, and customized services.

We ensure secure processing of your personal information, using it for the operation and administration of the website.

How we use your personal data includes (but is not limited to) the following purposes:

  • To personalize the customer experience
  • To create tailored ads and other promotional efforts
  • To provide you with quality services
  • To notify you of any changes to our service
  • To allow third-parties (when necessary) to perform logistical and technical functions in behalf of Clipping Path Studio
  • To implement our website Privacy Policy, Terms and Conditions, and other terms that are applicable to you
  • To help improve our future website development

If you have queries and concerns about this Privacy Policy or wish to withdraw your consent, contact us through phone (+088.....) or email (info@pictureclipingpath.com).

Any personal data we process is stored in a secure server, protected by a firewall. We do not retain said information longer than the necessary time it takes for processing.

We will only retain your personal data, such as your email address, if you consent to receive communications from us and only until you withdraw your consent.

Disclosure of Client Information to Third Parties

We may share your personal information with third-parties when necessary and in applicable situations.

Advertising Companies

Clipping Path Studio may use third-party advertisers to send you ads when you visit the website.

These advertising companies may use data about your visits to our website and other sites within web cookies. The information will be used to provide you with related products and services ads that might interest you.

Communications for Marketing

We may share your data with third party companies (as permitted by laws and regulations) for marketing purposes and only if you opt-in or give informed consent.

Service Providers

We might share your information with third-party providers that execute services for our company or on our behalf.

These data include email delivery, customer service, hosting services, payment processing, and more.

As Required by Law

We will release necessary client information to respond to legal processes, to remedy or investigate potential policy violations, and to protect the safety, rights, and property of others.

We may disclose your data as required by applicable regulations, laws, or rules. This includes giving necessary information about you to third parties for fraud protection.

We carefully screen third-parties we share client information with to ensure proper controls in place, such as GDPR compliant entities.

We do not sell your personal data and disclose only the information required for third-parties to deliver specific services.

We take appropriate measures to make sure that third parties keep your data secure and not use it for other reasons other than the intended purpose.

Image Copyright

We treat all the images you send to Clipping Path Studio as client property. We do not claim ownership of any photos and files that you upload and share with us.

We use secure servers to exchange files, and we do not expose client images to search engines and other methods of discovery.

However, we may use your submitted files for promotional purposes, such as publishing your images on our website as a sample project.

We will ask for permission before posting them for non-commercial purposes, and you can decline to give consent.

Please be advised that images sent under our Free Trial service are excluded from this scope of the Privacy Policy.

Data Security and Storage

We secure the personal information we hold under our company’s internal security policy and applicable data protection laws.

Internal Security

By submitting your private information, you agree to our storing and processing of client information.

We implement generally accepted standards of operational security and technology to protect personal information from unauthorized alteration, misuse, destruction, or loss.

However, we do not guarantee absolute protection since no digital storage method and transmission is 100% secure.

In the event of a security breach that will put your personal information at risk, we will take the necessary steps to prevent further damage and inform you promptly.

Data Protection Regulation

Clients are entitled to the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

  • The right to be informed
  • The right to access
  • The right to rectification
  • The right to erasure
  • The right to restrict processing
  • The right to data portability
  • The right to object
  • Rights concerning automated decision making and profiling

We commit to upholding the rights mentioned above. Please feel free to contact us if you wish to exercise your data protection rights.

Execute your right to withdraw any consent you provided Clipping Path Studio by contacting us directly via phone or email.

You can also opt-out of any electronic communications we send you by following the unsubscribe link within our messages.

We appreciate receiving feedback or questions you may have about how we process your personal data and our Privacy Policy. We’ll address your concerns as soon as possible.
