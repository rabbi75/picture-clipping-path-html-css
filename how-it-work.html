How It Works

It takes just 5 steps to get your images professionally edited by humans, not machines.

Here’s how to get your photos edited by the pros at Picture Clipping Path:

Step 1

Kick things off by
requesting a quote.

Head over to the quote request form and tell us what you need. Every project gets customized timing and pricing, so we’ll review the details to come up with yours.

Get your quote

Not a new customer? Skip this step if your needs are similar to projects you’ve submitted in the past.

Step 2

Customize and approve
your quote.

Within 45 minutes of requesting a quote, you’ll receive an email with the details of your order. Review and/or change the turnaround time and other project settings, then approve your quote.

Step 3

Upload your images via web browser
or BrickFTP (everything is secure).

We accept various file types:

  • JPG (preferred)
  • PSD
  • TIFF
  • PNG
  • NEF
  • RAW
  • CR2
  • DNG
  • PDF

Step 4

Relax (or get back to work)
while we edit your images.

Your images are carefully edited by hand — not some automated software. When they’re ready you’ll receive an email with a link to get your beautiful photos.

Step 5

Download your professionally
edited images.

Check out the finished product and download your final files. If you’re not happy with the result, we’re always more than happy to hear your feedback and adjust accordingly.
